Frequently Asked Questions

The app won't open or keeps crashing

Try these steps in order: (1) Force close the app and reopen it. (2) Restart your device. (3) Clear the app's cache in your device settings. (4) Uninstall and reinstall the app from the store. (5) Make sure your device's operating system is up to date.

How do I delete my account or data?

To request deletion of your account or personal data, please email me at darilquervo.dev@gmail.com with the subject line "Data Deletion Request". I will process your request within 7 business days.

I found a bug or something isn't working correctly

Please report it by email. Include a description of what happened, what you expected, your device model, and your OS version. Screenshots are very helpful.

I have a feature suggestion

I'd love to hear it! Send your idea to darilquervo.dev@gmail.com.
